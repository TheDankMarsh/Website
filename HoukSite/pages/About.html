History Of Company

Houk Masonry has been providing quality masonry services to the Walla Walla Valley and beyond for over several generations. Our team of seasoned masons has been honing their craft for decades, and we take pride in our work. We specialize in brick, block and stone masonry, as well as custom masonry projects. Our commitment to excellence and attention to detail has enabled us to build strong relationships with our customers and to develop a strong reputation within the community. It has been our privilege to of been able to work on prominent projects such as Walla Walla high school, McLoughlin High School, Walla Walla Hatchery and many more. We are proud to be a part of the Walla Walla Valley community and to be able to provide our services to our neighbors.

Our operations

We use only the best materials, and our masons are highly skilled and trained to use the tools and techniques necessary to complete each project with precision and accuracy. Our equipment operators are highly skilled and have years of experience in the trade. They understand the importance of safety and have a keen eye for detail. Our company has invested into Crank scaffolding, this is a type of scaffolding that uses a system of adjustable cranks to raise and lower the planks materials as well as our masons, allowing us to access higher areas more easily and with greater accuracy. This type of scaffolding is much more efficient than traditional scaffolding, as it can be adjusted in height automatically raher than having to move planks up by hand to each section. This allows us to complete commercial projects more quickly and efficiently, while also ensuring that our masonry projects are completed to the highest standards.
